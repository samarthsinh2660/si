## Narration Tips:

1. **Pace:** Speak clearly but confidently. Average 2.5-3 words per second.
2. **Emphasis:** Stress numbers (20+, 1000+, etc.) and key benefits (fast, reliable, trusted).
3. **Tone Shifts:**
   - Problem scene: slightly concerned
   - Solution scene: upbeat and reassuring
   - Stats/Trust: authoritative and proud
   - CTA: friendly but urgent
4. **Pauses:** Brief pause (0.5s) between scenes during transitions.
5. **Phone Number:** Pronounce clearly - "Nine eight two five one, double nine zero eight one"
